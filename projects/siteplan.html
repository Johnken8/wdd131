Site Name

Nigerian Cuisine Hub

This name was chosen to represent a comprehensive resource for Nigerian culinary traditions, recipes, and cultural context. It clearly communicates the focus on Nigerian food while the word "Hub" implies a central gathering place for information and community.

Optional domain availability: nigeriancuisinehub.com

Site Purpose

The Nigerian Cuisine Hub serves as a comprehensive resource for anyone interested in exploring Nigerian culinary traditions. The site provides:

  • Authentic Nigerian recipes with detailed step-by-step instructions
  • Information about regional Nigerian cooking styles and ingredients
  • Cultural context and history behind popular Nigerian dishes
  • A community forum for sharing cooking tips and experiences
  • Resources for finding Nigerian ingredients internationally

Scenarios

The following scenarios represent questions that potential visitors to the Nigerian Cuisine Hub might have:

  1. Question: "I'm interested in making Jollof Rice but don't know where to start. Can I find a beginner-friendly recipe with substitutions for ingredients that might be hard to find outside of Nigeria?"
  2. Question: "What are the key differences between Nigerian cuisine and other West African culinary traditions?"
  3. Question: "How can I learn about the cultural significance of Nigerian dishes like Egusi Soup and Fufu?"

Color Schema

#006B3F (Nigerian Green)

#FFFFFF (White)

#FF9A00 (Orange)

#333333 (Dark Gray)

Color Usage:

  • Nigerian Green (#006B3F): Primary color for header backgrounds, buttons, and accents. Represents the green in the Nigerian flag.
  • White (#FFFFFF): Background color for main content areas and text on dark backgrounds.
  • Orange (#FF9A00): Call-to-action buttons, highlights, and special features. Adds warmth and energy.
  • Dark Gray (#333333): Main text color and secondary backgrounds.

Typography

Playfair Display

Used for headings and titles

Roboto

Used for body text and navigation

Typography Usage:

  • Playfair Display: A serif font with elegant contrast used for headings (h1, h2, h3), titles, and feature text. It adds a sophisticated and traditional feel that complements the cultural richness of Nigerian cuisine.
  • Roboto: A clean, modern sans-serif font used for body text, navigation, and user interface elements. Its excellent readability makes it ideal for recipe instructions and informational content.
