Welcome to Nigeria

Nigeria, officially the Federal Republic of Nigeria, is a country in West Africa. It is the most populous country in Africa and the seventh most populous country in the world. Nigeria has a diverse geography, with climates ranging from arid to humid equatorial.

Known as the "Giant of Africa," Nigeria is a multicultural society with over 250 ethnic groups speaking more than 500 distinct languages. The three largest ethnic groups are the Hausa, Yoruba, and Igbo.

Key Statistics

Popular Attractions

Zuma Rock

A massive monolith located in Niger State, near the capital Abuja. It's one of Nigeria's most recognizable natural landmarks.

Lekki Conservation Centre

A nature reserve in Lagos with a canopy walkway, offering a unique perspective of the area's ecosystem.

Yankari Game Reserve

Nigeria's richest wildlife reserve, home to elephants, baboons, waterbucks, and various bird species.
